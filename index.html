About Parveen Sir

With over two decades of experience in the hair color industry, Parveen Sir has established himself as one of the leading hair color educators in the country. His expertise spans all aspects of professional hair coloring, from basic applications to advanced corrective techniques.

Having trained thousands of stylists across India, Parveen Sir is passionate about sharing his knowledge and helping professionals master the art and science of hair coloring.

His unique approach combines technical precision with artistic vision, ensuring beautiful, wearable color results that last.
